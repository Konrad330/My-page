Hi, I'm Konrad Gryboś. I design software that connects with medical devices.

Recent work

Asthma diary

Asthma Diary is an advanced app for monitoring asthma medication, offering tracking, reminders, reports, and device connectivity. I redesigned the app through research, evaluation, and prioritized updates. This included analyzing user needs, refining information architecture, standardizing design elements, and enhancing usability.

Smarthaler app

Smarthaler app connects with the FindAir Smarthaler, allowing users to manage inhaler functions, view its status, and record inhalation data via Bluetooth. I was responsible for designing the app, focusing on user-friendly interfaces, intuitive navigation, and a smooth connection experience.

Research app

Research application facilitates the process of connecting smart inhaler devices and transferring data to a viewing platform. I led the entire design process, from researching technical requirements and user needs to creating user flows, developing high-fidelity prototypes, and delivering final assets for development.

Doctor's platform

The Doctor’s Platform is a system for real-time inhalation monitoring in asthma and COPD treatment using smart inhalers. It allows doctors to track adherence, assess inhalation technique, and adjust therapy with data-driven insights. My work focused on data visualization, dashboards, navigation, and presenting insights to enhance decision-making.

About

Experience

With over five years of experience in UX design and research, I have worked primarily in startup environments, where adaptability and innovation are key. My expertise spans medical platforms, mobile applications, and e-commerce solutions, with a focus on complex systems that demand thoughtful problem-solving and user-centered design.Throughout my career, I have contributed to medical device certifications, conducted usability studies, and designed laboratory tests to ensure compliance with industry standards. Collaborating with external clients, I have led demos and workshops while effectively documenting and presenting my designs. A deep understanding of development processes enables me to navigate system constraints and deliver effective solutions. In addition to my design work, I also specialize in building websites using Webflow and Framer,

Design principles

Working in the field of medical devices, I am guided by core design principles that emphasize clarity, practicality, and user-centered solutions. I strive to create designs that first and formost meet functional needs.  Recognizing the critical role medical devices play, I approach my work with careful attention to detail, ensuring every aspect—from the design itself to the language I use—is precise and effective. While I value aesthetics, I prioritize clarity and usability, always seeking a balance between beauty and functionality. My designs consider larger systems and their interactions, ensuring they seamlessly integrate into their environments to help users achieve their goals.
